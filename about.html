FAQ

The questions people actually ask

Will an AI be spending my ad budget unsupervised?

No. AI handles monitoring, analysis and proposals. Any change that spends money — budget, bid, status, price — waits for a human to approve it. Daily automation is read-only and loss-prevention only.

Do I have to move my store or accounts to you?

Never. Everything runs in your own Shopify, Google Ads and analytics accounts. You grant access; you can revoke it any time. Nothing is locked inside a tool only I control.

Can I start with just one thing?

Yes — most clients do. Pick the pillar that hurts most (often ads monitoring or listing), prove the value, then add the next. No big-bang commitment.

What size of store is this for?

Stores with enough catalog or ad spend that manual work is the bottleneck. If you're hand-listing products or eyeballing campaigns daily, automation pays for itself fast. Very early-stage stores may be better served by simpler fixes — I'll say so in the audit.

Do you guarantee a specific ROAS or ranking?

No one honest does. I guarantee systems that are correctly built, safely automated and transparently reported. The numbers follow from a good operation — and I'll show you exactly what's happening either way.
