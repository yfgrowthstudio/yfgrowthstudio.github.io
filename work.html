Case Studies

Systems I designed and shipped — running in production.

These were built as GM and co-founder of an Australian cross-border e-commerce group. Client names and internal details are anonymized; the architecture, scale and approach are real.

Pillar 01 · Automated Listing

From 60,000-SKU catalog to live listings, on a schedule

The problem

A multi-store operation with a 60k+ SKU supplier catalog. Selecting what to list, writing content and pricing it was manual, slow, and impossible to keep current across stores.

What I built

An agent pipeline that runs weekly catalog scans, scores candidates by fit, then bulk-publishes to Shopify with generated titles, descriptions and categories — each priced against a cost-based floor so nothing lists below margin.

Pillar 02 · SEO

A 22-tool SEO workbench, organized by stage

The problem

SEO work was scattered and inconsistent: keyword research disconnected from real demand, category pages unoptimized, no repeatable monthly motion, and AI-drafted content risked going live unchecked.

What I built

A single workbench of 22 tools across 7 stages — keyword research validated against real search volume, category-page optimization, content, backlinks, Google Business Profile and monthly reporting — with approval-gated publishing so nothing ships without a human sign-off.

Pillar 03 · Creative

Product & ad visuals generated to spec

The problem

Campaigns and listings constantly waited on design. Every ad format needs different aspect ratios; every product needs white-background, lifestyle and detail shots. Design was the bottleneck.

What I built

An automated visual-asset pipeline that produces product-page image sets and ad creative in every required format (1:1, 1.91:1, 9:16), on-brand by template, with headline and description variants ready for A/B testing.

Pillar 04 · Ads

Real-time Google Ads monitoring across 9 storefronts

The problem

Nine storefronts under one manager account meant a daily manual slog to catch wasted spend — and small problems (a campaign tanking, a zero-conversion budget burn) hid until they cost real money.

What I built

One live dashboard pulling campaign data straight from the Google Ads API, with four-bucket anomaly detection — low ROAS, low CTR, zero-conversion spend and abnormal status — flagging problems automatically. Plus the full build pipeline: account, GA4, GTM and Merchant Center, Search and PMax, on a weekly optimization cadence.

The platform underneath

100+ tools, orchestrated by AI agents

The idea

None of the four pillars is a standalone hack. They run on a shared internal platform of 100+ API tools, dispatched to Claude-based agents — so listing, SEO, creative and ads draw on the same data and the same guardrails.
